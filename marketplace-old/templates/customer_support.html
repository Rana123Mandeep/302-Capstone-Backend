Frequently Asked Questions

How do I contact a seller?

+

You can contact a seller by visiting the product page and clicking on the "Contact Seller" button. This will allow you to send a message directly to the seller regarding the product.

How do I list an item for sale?

+

To list an item for sale, click on the "Sell" button in the navigation bar. You'll need to provide details about your item, including a title, description, price, category, condition, and at least one photo. Once submitted, your item will be live on the marketplace.

How do payments work?

+

We recommend cash payments upon in-person pickup. For safety, we suggest meeting in public places for exchanges. The marketplace does not currently process online payments directly.

What should I do if I suspect a scam?

+

If you suspect a scam, please report it immediately using the contact form below. Include as many details as possible about the listing or communication. We take fraud very seriously and will investigate promptly.

How do I delete my account?

+

To delete your account, please contact our support team using the form below. Note that deleting your account will remove all your listings and messages permanently.
